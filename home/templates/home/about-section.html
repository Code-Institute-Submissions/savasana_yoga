About Savasana Yoga

Yoga promotes a present state of mind, to focus on breathing, and the importance of being mindful. It's a fantastic form of exercise, which can improve flexibility, and balance.

Savasana Yoga offers a variety of classes for all difficulty levels. Whether it's your first time, or you're an advanced yogi, we've got a class for you! Experienced, and compassionate teachers, provide full attention to your needs, limitations and goals.
